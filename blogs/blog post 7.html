MILANI MEDIA HOUSE

A TYPICAL DAY IN A JOZI STUDIO

Posted 04 July 2021 by Phila Hillie

I was recently able to work with Oglivy, a talented and well known advertising agency in Johannesburg, for on a photoshoot for branding and advertising for a local campaign for MTV. The brief was to create animation and a cohesive overall look for a Women's day campaign in August. I was brought on as a freelance graphic designer and also as an artist who will deliver a message for Women's day to motivate South African women.

The photoshoot was held at SunshineCo. which is situated in the Keyes Art Mile in Johannesburg. I am so excited to be a part of a campaign that aligns with the work that I do and also gives me a platform as an artist. The photoshoot was done in a green screen style because it was going to be overlayed with animation that will be done by myself and and upcoming female artists.
