THE ORIGIN SERIES - BEHIND THE SCENES

20 April 2021

by Phila Hillie

As an African woman, I have always had a weird relationship with where I am from and where my family is from. It is weird becasue you think that it would be the same place but that is not the case. I was born and raised in Johannesburg with elements and cntributions from the Eastern Cape while my family was born and raised in the Eastern Cape.

"The Origin Series" is my exploration of where home is and how I have navigated the reality of calling two places home. I feel like collaging will help illustrate the contrast and juxtaposition of my two homes and the texture will illustrate the "reality".

I used images of the ocean because I LOVE the ocean and feel a special connection with the water and energy of the waves. I also used images of the Johannesburg bridges and considered using the skyline because that is what I felt best represented Johannesburg.
