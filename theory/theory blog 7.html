CHARACTER RATIONALE

Posted 16 April 2021 by Phila Hillie

My character is Mila and she is trying to start a company called "Milani Media House", a multimedia design house in which she shares her creative process as an artist, her growing career and participation in projects in Johannesburg and the research she has done along the way. She is based in Johannesburg and her work explores the intersection between growing up in Johannesburg while having cultural roots in the rural Eastern Cape.

Promoting her work and creating a safe digital space for women of colour is important for her. She does this through discussing topics that affect women of colour and also by giving practical insight and advice for people looking to pursue a career in art and digital art. She is passionate about women and women of colour taking up more physical space.

My characterâ€™s necessity was born out of the need to have digital designers who understand, care and cater for the needs and interests of women of colour who may have access to online content but have not been given the same platform and opportunities to contribute to online content and represent themselves through their own eyes as opposed to through the gaze of people looking into their community. She produces content and also advises her viewers on the challenges that come with creating content and websites.

Mila wants to shed light on her experiences as an up and coming artist in the South African work environment and in the Johannesburg art scene. Her art is inspired by her Xhosa culture and diverse and unique Johannesburg energy that she grew up in.

"Milani" means "firm in faith" in isiXhosa and this speaks to the objectives of the design house and to Mila as a person as it is her full name. Mila has faith in herself as an artist and in women and the stories they have to tell.
