WIREFRAMES

29 May 2021

After working on my website for the past month, I realised that my design needed more of my work incorporated into it so that viewers could connect with a body of work and with me as a person. I decided to introduce 'moments' to my website. These 'moments' would be image sliders/carousels that would have images of my artworks and the events that I have participated in.
