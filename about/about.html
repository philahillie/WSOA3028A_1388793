ABOUT MILANI MEDIA HOUSE

Hi, my name is Mila

I am a young Johannesburg-based African multimedia design graduate trying to break into a make dominated industry. I am passionate about storytelling from the perspective of women and providing authentic stories about women by women

I am looking to champion and shed light on women and explore how narrative, different art mediums, lighting and camera techniques can enhance them and their stories.

I like to sketch out my storyboards before translating them into photographs, collages and sometimes film. I will be sharing them and letting you in on my creative process from the conception to the final product so you can maybe share my love for the magic of creating beautiful and authentic stories.

Milani means to be firm in faith in isiXhosa and I believe that this is a beautiful manifestation to base my website on. Although the idea of putting myself out there is daunting, I believe in this work and hope you will too.

I hope you get to enjoy this journey with me and that we can create connections - between you and me and you and my work - along the way.

I look forward to growing through each project and interacton. This site was made with love and I hope you continue to feel that love throughout.

With love and gratitude,

Mila
